Thank you for participating in our experiment!

The purpose of this study was to investigate how musical phrases influence sentence production. We presented you with different musical phrases to determine what harmonic patterns influenced your sentence completions. The results of the study will inform our understanding of the relationship between language and music.

All of the information collected during your participation is confidential and will not be linked back to you. If you have any questions about this research you can contact Jacob Prescott or Umass Amherst’s Institutional Review Board with the contact information listed below.
